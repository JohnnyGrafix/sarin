Sarin gas: a deadly nerve agent

Originally developed as a pesticide during the 1930s Nazi regime in Germany

Odorless, colorless and tasteless. Evaporates into a vapor when exposed to air

Can be inhaled or absorbed through skin

Heavier than air, lingers for hours

Outlawed under the 1993 Chemical Weapons Convention

Produced in the past by the US and former Soviet Union
